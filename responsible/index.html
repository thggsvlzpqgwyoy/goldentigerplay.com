Recognizing Problem Gambling

It's important to be aware of the signs of problem gambling. Ask yourself the following questions:

  • Do you spend more money or time gambling than you can afford?
  • Do you try to win back losses with more gambling?
  • Does gambling interfere with your work, studies, or personal life?
  • Do you feel restless or irritable when trying to cut down on gambling?
  • Have you lied to hide the extent of your gambling?

If you answered yes to any of these questions, or if you are concerned about your gambling habits, we strongly encourage you to seek help.
